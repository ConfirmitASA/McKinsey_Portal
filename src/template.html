About Employee Listening Hub

The Employee Listening Hub applies data and rigor to the most important organizational decisions.

Sustained performance depends more than ever on the ability to manage, align, and grow organizations. Too often, critical organizational decisions are made on the basis of instinct and personal bias. The Employee Listening Hub solves that problem by bringing “hard” data, analytics, and technology to the “soft” art of organizational leadership.
